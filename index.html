How it works

One platform, two sides

🏠
For providers
1
Create your profile — list your home or services, availability, rates, certifications, and languages spoken.
2
Get discovered — families in your area search and find you based on their care needs and preferences.
3
Connect and fill beds — receive inquiries, message families directly, and confirm placements in the platform.
❤️
For families
1
Describe your needs — tell us about your loved one's care requirements, location, budget, and preferences.
2
Browse verified providers — search licensed AFH homes and vetted caregivers with real reviews and credentials.
3
Find the right fit — message providers, schedule tours, and make confident placement decisions.
The problem

Finding quality AFH care is broken

Families searching for adult family home placement have no good way to compare options. Word of mouth, outdated state directories, and cold calls to homes — that's the current process. It's slow, stressful, and opaque.

Providers on the other side have no modern way to market available beds or reach the families that need them most.

🔍
No central place to search and compare AFH providers
📋
State directories are outdated and hard to navigate
🏠

⏳
Placement takes weeks — families need answers faster
